The Best Anime According to Higashi

My top 3 Anime of all-time.

One Piece

Embarking on the epic voyage that is One Piece is an experience unlike any other in the realm of anime and manga. Spanning over two decades and still sailing strong, this remarkable series has captured the hearts and imaginations of millions worldwide, and it's easy to see why. With its rich storytelling, compelling characters, and boundless sense of adventure, One Piece has established itself as an absolute masterpiece.

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is well-known for its art style and poses, frequent references to Western popular music and fashion, and battles centered around Stands, psycho-spiritual manifestations with unique supernatural abilities.

Rent-A-Girlfriend

"Rent a Girlfriend" is a whirlwind of romance that sweeps viewers off their feet from the very first episode. With its clever premise and charming characters, this anime delivers a romantic rollercoaster ride that leaves hearts aflutter. The relationship dynamics between Kazuya and his rented girlfriend, Chizuru, are as hilarious as they are heartwarming, filled with moments of laughter, longing, and unexpected tenderness.
